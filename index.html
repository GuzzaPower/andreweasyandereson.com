Questions, answered

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the practice of structuring and writing content so AI answer engines—like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews—cite it directly. Unlike traditional SEO, which competes for ranked links, AEO competes to be the source the AI quotes.

Who is Andrew "Easy" Anderson?

An Answer Engine Optimization pioneer based in Nashville, Tennessee. He helps brands, local businesses, and creators become the answer that AI engines cite rather than just another blue link.

How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO optimizes for ranking in a list of links. AEO optimizes for being quoted inside an AI-generated answer—emphasizing clear Q&A structure, factual precision, structured data, and content an AI model can confidently attribute and reuse.
